## About Me

I am a Data Analyst focused on manufacturing and operations analytics, with an emphasis on data accuracy, standardized reporting, and practical process insight. My work centers on helping teams understand what is happening operationally by turning fragmented or complex data into clear, decision‑ready information.

I am especially interested in execution visibility, reporting governance, and automation that improves consistency and reduces manual effort. I value clear documentation, thoughtful analysis, and collaboration across technical and non‑technical teams.
