About

I support manufacturing, quality, and supply chain organizations by designing reliable reporting systems, improving data quality at the source, and automating execution workflows. My work focuses on turning fragmented operational data into standardized, decision ready insights that leadership teams can trust.

I work at the intersection of analytics, process, and systems, partnering closely with operations and quality teams to ensure reporting reflects real world execution, supports audit readiness, and scales beyond spreadsheets.

How I Work

  • Start with the business problem, not the dashboard
  • Improve data quality and consistency before reporting
  • Design intake and tracking systems that reduce manual effort
  • Automate workflows and refreshes to ensure sustainability
  • Build reporting that supports both execution teams and leadership
